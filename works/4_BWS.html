Description

We need to ‘take urgent and significant action to protect and prevent the extinction of threatened species’ according to the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

But what species are threatened? Does it depend where they live? Is there anything we can do about it?

The world’s most comprehensive information source on threatened species –
The International Union for Conservation of Nature’s Red List – can help us find out.

This project using this dataset and insight from Iucnredlist.org.
